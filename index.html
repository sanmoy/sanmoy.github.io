Classic Triangle Puzzle Game

Overview

At Nascent Software, we craft thoughtful and entertaining mobile games designed to spark joy and challenge your mind. We're excited to introduce "Zen Triangle," our latest puzzle adventure—now ready for you to play!

"Zen Triangle" is a timeless puzzle for everyone. The gameplay is vibrant, intuitive, and engaging.

Inspired by a classic Chinese puzzle, this unique game is perfect for all ages, from 8 to 80.

The rules are simple: assemble 12 colorful pieces to complete a triangle. You can rotate, flip, and move the pieces to find their correct positions. Some pieces are symmetric and cannot be rotated, while others are mirror asymmetric and can only be flipped.

Play the tutorial to master the basic moves, then dive into the main game. With 350 stages, "Zen Triangle" will keep you thinking for months!

This addictive logic puzzle is a great way for parents and kids to play together. It’s both educational and fun—helping children learn how different objects fit together.
